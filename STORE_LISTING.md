# GDrive Sidebar Pinner - Store Listing

## Summary (One-liner)
A premium sidebar widget for Google Drive that lets you pin folders for instant access with color preservation.

## Detailed Description
Supercharge your Google Drive navigation with **GDrive Sidebar Pinner**. 

Do you find yourself constantly navigating deep into folder structures? Tired of hunting for your most-used project directories? GDrive Sidebar Pinner injects a native-looking "Pinned" section directly into your Google Drive sidebar, right below the Starred section.

### Key Features:
- **Instant Access**: Pin any folder to your sidebar for one-click navigation.
- **Color Preservation**: Automatically detects your custom folder colors and matches them in the sidebar icons for visual clarity.
- **Optimized for Multi-Tab Workflows**: Folders open in new tabs by default, keeping your primary Drive view intact.
- **Seamless Integration**: Designed with Google's Material 3 aesthetics. It feels like a native part of Google Drive.
- **Fast & Lightweight**: Built with performance in mind. Uses an intelligent MutationObserver that won't slow down your browser.
- **Cross-Device Sync**: Your pinned folders sync across any browser where you're logged into your Google account.

### How to use:
1. Navigate into any folder in Google Drive.
2. Click the floating "Pin Folder" button in the bottom-right corner.
3. Your folder is now pinned to the sidebar!
